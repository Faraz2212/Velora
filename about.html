Our Story

How Velora Interiors began and evolved over the years

As fellow graduate students at MIT in 2004, Brian and Dhamneh noticed a shift in the way people interact with their environments. People were no longer tolerating interruptive designs for their attention – in fact, they'd gotten really selective about their surroundings.

This insight led to the founding of Velora Interiors in 2010, with a mission to create spaces that are both beautiful and functional, where design serves people rather than interrupts them.
